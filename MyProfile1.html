About me

Born in D.R.C., one of the Richest Countries in the world in terms of raw mineral resources, with the largest cobalt reserves in the world, at some 3.6 million metric tones as of 2019. While my Country is one of the poorest in the world in relation to the quality of life, wealth creation opportunities because of poor management, established corruption, and lack of accountability; I strive to pursue excellence. I currently live in Johannesburg, South Africa. I am an Entrepreneur, a Scholar pursuing a Master's degree in Business Adminsitration, an Author, an International Speaker, and a Web Designer. I am married, and have two children.

I have studied with the University of Michigan through Colleen Van Lent how to code with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScrip. In return, I want to use this skill and many others that I have acquired to empower the youth across Africa through my Nonprofit Organzation. It is a way for me to give back to the communities, and eraducate poverty among the youth of Africa by emporing them to become entrepreneurs so that they do not rely on governments.

Director @ Destiny Stars Investments (Pty) Ltd

As co-founder and director of Destiny Stars Investments, I facilitate investments into African Countries. I source commodities, mainly gold and diamonds that I supply to different buyers in the Middle-East, particularly Oman, and to the United States. I also raise funds and identify potential investors for cooperatives in mining communities to assist small scale miners with mining equipment, and selling their products to the right buyers. In partnership with Lone Star Commodities in DRC, we are planning to empower mining communities with skills in Information Technology, the Fourth Industrial Revolution, and Sustainable Agriculture to create diversity in the local economies.
